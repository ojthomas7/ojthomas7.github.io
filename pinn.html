Overview

This project extends upon the two previous projects o the same theme. Previously, I have constructed a classical neural network to classify the principle quantum state of images of the electron density about the hydrogen nucleus. I extended this work by incorporating a physics informed loss function in order to both improve the models accuracy and extend its classification abilities into the angular and magnetic quantum states l and m instead of simply the principle quantum number n.

  • A physics-informed loss function was incorporated
  • The training data was improved by generating images from n = 1 to n = 9, increasing the size of the training data set.
  • The model was proven to be more accurate, most often correctly classifying l, the angular quantum number.
