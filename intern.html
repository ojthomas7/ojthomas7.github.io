Overview

I held the position of a research intern under Dr. Calum Williams with the physics department at the University of Exeter. I worked primarily on hardware control, my objective being to create software that communicated with each individual piece of hardware in the lab. This was completed by working with each components SDK, eventually culminating in a singular python file with a GUI that allowed researchers to automate and conduct their experiments remotely. My work was well received and is currently being used in research exploring hyper-spectral imaging for medical purposes.
